Host an Event

Fill out our Event Form if you'd like to host a meetup, training, party, fundraiser, exhibition, or some other kind of event at Liminal.

Hold a Workshop

Liminal is a free space to hold educational workshops for members of the local community.

  • You do not need professional certification of any sort in order to teach a workshop.
  • All workshop entry fees must be made pay-what-you-can.
  • You may choose either to keep the proceeds for yourself, or to donate them to Liminal.

To propose a workshop you would like to host at Liminal, fill out our Workshop Form.
