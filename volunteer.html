Why Volunteer at Liminal?

Liminal is completely run by volunteers. All revenue that we generate goes directly into paying for the costs of the space and the improvement of our programs.

It is important to us that all volunteers are made to be integral and equal team members. Since we do not believe that any one person's labor is more valuable than another, there is no difference in compensation between any working position.

Just as each person plays a small role in making a strong community, Liminal volunteers contribute their individual time and unique abilities to make the space function effectively. All staff positions are geared around an equal time commitment of 4-8 hours per week. Similarly, cafe volunteers are required to commit to a minimum of one 4 hour weekly shift.

For prospective volunteers who aren't able to make a weekly commitment, we always welcome drop-ins from people who want to help with cleaning tasks or distributing event fliers.

Submit an Application

All interested persons must apply by submitting a brief piece of writing that addresses the following questions:

  • What would you like to tell us about yourself?
  • Why are you interested in volunteering at Liminal in general?
  • What role(s) are you most interested in taking on? (up to 2 roles)
  • Why do you think you would be a good fit for the position?

No formal experience is required to apply for any role. However, resumes are appreciated if you would like to provide one.

Submit your response as a PDF document to liminal.flagstaff@gmail.com along with any other supplemental materials you would like us to see.

If you have any questions, send us an email and we'll get back to you as soon as possible!
