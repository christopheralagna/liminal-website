Food and Beverage will be in operation at a later date.
Liminal will serve food and drink during open hours. It is absolutely not necessary to make any purchases in order to use our space.

A Note on Payment

Liminal will have a pay high // pay low system in place.

Having expendable funds is both an uncertainty // unrealistic for many folks in our community, thus we have established a community equity model. If you can “pay high” for a meal // a drink // an event- the overcharge you provide will go directly into covering the cost for those who can only afford to “pay low”!
