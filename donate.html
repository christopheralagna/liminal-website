DONATE / VOLUNTEER

Monetary Donations

All facets of Liminal are free to access or pay-what-you-can, including the space, its material resources, and all our events. We are completely funded through community donations. Therefore, we encourage that those of you who are able to contribute choose to do so. We really appreciate it!

Other Contributions

Non-monetary contributions are equally as important as monetary donations. All of the material resources that we freely provide during our open hours are donated by community members. Furthermore, most of our public amenities are serviced or maintained through volunteer labor.

Here is a brief list of material donations and other contributions that we are always seeking:

  • books
  • clothing - our unsheltered communities especially appreciate warm clothes, underwear, socks, sleeping bags, blankets, rain gear, and backpacks. All other sorts of clothes are also accepted.
  • art supplies
  • stationery items and working materials - such as pens, pencils, paper, notebooks, binders, staplers, push pins, erasers, highlighters, dry erase markers, and so on.
  • cleaning supplies
  • skills - such as carpentry, electrical assistance, plumbing assistance, and general maintenance.

If there is something else you are interested in donating which is not listed above, please reach out to us!

Volunteering

All of Liminal is completely run by volunteers. Whether you are able to volunteer consistently or only occasionally, we are always needing your help!

Volunteer Groups

Liminal has several different volunteer groups who tend to different types of tasks which help keep the space running. Our current volunteering groups include:

  • Cafe Shifts
  • Event Coordination
  • Flyer Making
  • Flyer Distribution
  • Photography and Video Recording
  • Cleaning and Organizing
  • Fundraising
  • Social Media
  • Plant Care
  • Carpentry

If you’d like to join our team of volunteers, fill out our volunteer form. Afterwards, you will automatically be sent an email with details about how to move forward!
