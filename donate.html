DONATE / VOLUNTEER

Monetary Donations

We are completely funded through community donations. All facets of Liminal are free to access including the space, its material resources, and all of our events. Therefore we ask that those of you who are able to contribute, do so.

You can donate by visiting our Venmo.

Other Donations

We are always accepting and encouraging material donations, including...

  • radical books
  • clothing - our unsheltered communities especially appreciate warm clothes, underwear, socks, sleeping bags, blankets, rain gear, and backpacks. All other sorts of clothes are also accepted.
  • art supplies
  • stationery items and working materials - such as pens, pencils, paper, notebooks, binders, staplers, push pins, erasers, highlighters, dry erase markers, and so on.
  • cleaning supplies
  • skills - such as carpentry, electrical assistance, plumbing assistance, and general maintenance.

Reach out and ask us if you’re wanting to give anything else you think might be of use.

Volunteering

Our space is run entirely by volunteers. This includes those who help with project coordination, interior work and renovation, teaching, outreach, cleaning, and so on.

We especially need ongoing help from people willing to contribute their time watching over the space, so that Liminal can be open as often as possible. Offering your time in this way allows queer and marginalized folx to have more consistent access to the resources that Liminal provides. We are currently working to create an online schedule where those interested can sign up for volunteering time slots.

Until we formalize a schedule, please reach out to us directly if you have any interest in volunteering.
